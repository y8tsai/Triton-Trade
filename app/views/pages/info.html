About Triton Trade

Triton Trade is a web application that is desgined as an online marketplace for UCSD students. Once their accounts have been registered, users can sign in to buy, sell, and trade a wide variety of items with other users. With this application, users will have an organized and secure platform to perform these transactions. This application also contains conevnient features such as searching for items and browsing by category in order to improve the user's experience. Happy trading, Tritons!

Team OBT
